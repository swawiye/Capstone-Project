Hear some voices from our community

Here are some testimonies of people's lives which have been transformed while going through the Metanoia experience

I learnt my personal value

I used to struggle with my self-esteem , aside from having a poor self-perception. I was able to realise how valuable I am as a person. This awakening catalysed a butterfly effect in my life, that had an immense positive impact.

My though process was changed

Normally, my thought process comprised of a linear thought process that was hard to degress from. As a result, this mindset bloomed into perpetual negativity that prevented me from achieving my goals. Manifestation of your dreams begins in the mind- I'm glad I learnt that.

Self-love is important

Five years ago, when my husband took off and left me, my persona was left damaged; much more than anything else. My life was literally falling apart. I blamed myself for that, but after some time I realised that I had lost the love for myself. No one will ever love you as much as you love yourself.

Conner Hendrew
